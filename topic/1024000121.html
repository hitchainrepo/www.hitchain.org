HitChain Foundation Was Invited to Participate in The Finastra Universe Conference

March 28, 2019

Recently, the HitChain Foundation was invited to participate in the Finastra Universe Singapore conference. This Finastra Universe conference brought together more than 500 outstanding leaders and experts across Asia Pacific financial sector. The theme for 2019 Finastra Universe will be Collaborate to Innovate, focusing on the new shape of disruption: how academia, Fintech, software and banks are collaborating as one, to develop fintech and global financial services. The HitChain team and many outstanding representatives from the Asia-Pacific financial community have in-depth discussions on the application prospects and innovative development models of blockchain technology.
