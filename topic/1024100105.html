HitChain Project Biweekly Report 5 November—16 November, 2018

November 16, 2018

1. News Review

1) On the afternoon of November 9, HitChain operation team was invited to participate in the second salon event hosted by Cointime, “Cointime on the Open Source of Eco-Digital Wallet”. Our team believes that wallet open source of digital wallets is an inevitable trend, but it needs to be carried out step by step. HitChain focuses on serving open source projects, providing decentralized code hosting, and could deeply cooperate with the open source code of digital wallets.

2）On September 26, HitChain's senior chief architect Tyler Chen attended the BlockDAO product launch conference. At the conference, Cloud Town Management Committee, BlockDAO, Blockchain Research Center in School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science Peking University, LD Research, BlockLabs and many other enterprises and institutions jointly launched the Yunxi Blockchain Industry Development Association. In addition, BlockDAO has also established an incubator——DAOSpace to support the blockchain quality project. HitChain, as the first representative of the incubator project, will accelerate the application of technology and improve the ecological construction with the assistance of BlockDAO.

2. Project Progress

1) The Basic Procedure of Storage Confirmation：

a、Clients regularly report the storage information (continuously online over 30 minutes)：Prevent from tampering client information through zero knowledge verification；

b、Server periodically check clients’ service condition：Check the clients’ online status；

c、Server’s abnormal data detection service：Regularly check client’s report information and the periodic check information (through the time interval and the check result to analyze the abnormal behavior).

2) Implement the client’s regular report function.

3) Implement the server’s regular check function.

4) Modify the way of permission authentication, which makes hit support branch operation.

5) Implement the compatibility test of windows, including:

a、hit push；

b、hit transfer；

c、hit pull；

d、hit clone；

e、hit commit.

6) Convert some Scala code to Java code to extend functions.

7) Improve HIT storage protocol and implement a part of the Index specification.

8) Research and design the interaction of the DAPP and HIT storage.
