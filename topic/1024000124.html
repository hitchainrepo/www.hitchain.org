HitChain Foundation Was Invited to Participate in The 2019 Global Blockchain (Hangzhou) Summit Forum hosted by Hangzhou Financial Office

May 17, 2019

On May 17, the 2019 Global Blockchain (Hangzhou) Summit Forum hosted by Hangzhou Financial Office was officially opened. The theme of the forum is “Embracing the Commercial Age and Empowering the Digital Economy”, and invited many experts at home and abroad to bring the most cutting-edge global developments and the most profound blockchain ideas. The HitChain team actively participated in the blockchain industry event and discussed with a group of top domestic teams to meet the commercial era of blockchain. HitChain is a global ecosystem of Open-source applications based on an Open-source philosophy and Blockchain technology. HitChain is committed to building a business system where developers build together, serve developers, and make developers profitable. It can protect the rights of developers while giving new energy to the digital economy.
