Open Source technology Expert Chen Zhao Joined HitChain

August 30, 2018

Recently, open source technologist Chen Zhao joined the HitChain development team as a senior architect. As a full-time senior architect at the HitChain Foundation, he will be responsible for the technical work of the distributed architecture of the HitChain code-managed system. Chen Zhao has 13 years of experience in R&D and delivery of large enterprise application systems and 12 years of experience in utilizing open source technologies and open source solutions. Before joining the HitChain Foundation, he has served as the chief architect of Foreveross, a well-known open source software company. He has performed 10million yuan level open source service for China International Airlines, China Southern Airlines, Hainan Airlines and Changan Automobile. He is committed to promoting open source technology applications and open source solutions. HitChain is a decentralized, autonomous community powered by blockchain of the developers, by the developers, for the developers. Chen Zhao's participation will promote the rapid landing of the HitChain project.
