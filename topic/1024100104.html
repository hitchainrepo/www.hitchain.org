HitChain Project Biweekly Report 20 October, 2018——4 November, 2018

November 5, 2018

Hitchain senior architect Tyler Chen took the technical team to participate in the 2018 China Computer Conference in Hangzhou. HitChain (HIT) successfully launched the USDT zone of the Huobi Global at 15:00 on November 1st and list on HIT/USDT trading.

1. News Review

1) From October 25th to 26th, Hitchain senior architect Tyler Chen took the technical team to participate in the 2018 China Computer Conference in Hangzhou. During the conference, Mr. Chen Zhao introduced HitChain's product vision, design concept, underlying architecture and current project progress to computer experts and blockchain peers and established relationship networks with famous universities such as Tsinghua University, Zhejiang University and Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
