HitChain Foundation Was Invited to Participate in The MiiX·Axi Global Blockchain Hackathon Marathon

April 28, 2019

From April 27th to 28th, HitChain senior architect Chen Zhao was invited as a judge to participate in the MiiX·Axi Global Blockchain Hackathon Marathon. The competition attracted 25 professional teams from online and offline to participate actively. The participating teams jointly explored the future of autonomous community building and the innovative application of blockchain. The HitChain technical team also has technical exchanges with participating geeks. By participating in this competition, it will help the developer autonomous community on the blockchain - HitChain to better serve developers.

From April 26th to 28th, the HitChain technical team participated in the 2050 conference with the theme of “Tech Brings Youth Together”. This conference convened tens of thousands of “youth” who love technology. The conference has 100 youth reunions, 100 new forums and 127 black technology booths. At the meeting, the HitChain team discussed the application and development direction of the blockchain with many young people who loved the blockchain, providing more possibilities for the construction of the HitChain developer community.
